Hi! I am a first-year CS PhD student at the [University of Maryland, College Park](https://www.cs.umd.edu), advised by [Heng Huang](https://www.cs.umd.edu/~heng/). I am also a Graduate Research Assistant at the [University of Maryland Institute for Health Computing (UM-IHC)](https://www.ihc.umd.edu/), where I bridge expertise in machine learning and computer vision with imaging informatics to revolutionize everyday clinical practice and improve patient outcomes. 

My research is focused on enabling opportunistic screening for early-stage disease detection and accelerating clinical decision-making for life-threatening diseases. Currently, I am advancing this field through: 
1. **Multimodal foundation models** that integrate imaging, clinical, and multi-omics data for disease detection.
2. **Generative models** that use diffusion- and Flow Matching-based methods for counterfactual generation, data augmentation, and accelerated series acquisition.
 
In my free time, I enjoy hiking, gardening, and reading about history, biogeography, and urban planning!
